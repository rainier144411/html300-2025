Types of Meals

Lighter options to keep you going between meals.

Meals that require no stove and are great options for lunch along the trail.

Simple meals require minimal preparation, and you just need to boil water when you get to camp. They are a great option for single-night backpacking trips.

Dehydrated meals take a good bit of preparation at home but make for lighter packs. They are the best option for multi-night treks.
